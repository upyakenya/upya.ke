About UPYA

Uwezo Pamoja Youths Association (UPYA) is a youth-led community organisation focused on leadership, mentorship, community projects and creating opportunities for the youth to grow and impact society.

Mission

To empower, equip, mentor and nurture tomorrow's world.

Vision

Creating an environment that cultivates positive impact to the community.

Our Objectives & Core Values

Teamwork • Integrity • Transparency • Accountability • Equity

Empowerment
Build skills, confidence and opportunities for youth.
Mentorship
Guidance from experienced leaders and peers.
Community Impact
Projects that create real improvement in people’s lives.
Innovation
Support creative and practical youth-led solutions.
Collaboration
Work with partners for sustainable change.
Equity
Fair access and opportunity for all youths.
