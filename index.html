    About Us

    Uwezo Pamoja Youths Association (UPYA) is dedicated to empowering, equipping, mentoring, and nurturing tomorrow’s world. We strive to create an environment that cultivates positive impact in the community.

    History

    Founded recently by a group of motivated youth leaders to unite and empower the community through practical programs and mentorship.

    Mission

    To empower, equip, mentor, and nurture tomorrow's world.

    Vision

    Creating an environment that cultivates positive impact in the community.

    Our Objectives

    • ❤️To provide mentorship and guidance to young people in the community.
    • ❤️To nurture talents and create opportunities for innovation.
    • ❤️To engage in community development and environmental sustainability projects.
    • ❤️To promote teamwork, integrity, and accountability among members.

    Our Core Values

    Teamwork
    Integrity & Transparency
    Accountability & Equity
